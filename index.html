How Do We Structure the Day?

1. Training

We teach all the necessary things you need to know to jump from the plane without any problems!

2. Get Ready

You are already prepared, you just need the suit and parachute. All sizes available.

3. Fly

You are ready, and the plane is waiting for us in the hangar. Let's fly!

4. Jump!

You have done the most complicated parts. Just one step left...JUMP!
